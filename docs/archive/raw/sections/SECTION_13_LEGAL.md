# Section 13 — Legal, Policy & Compliance (Full Runbook)

**Objective:** Finalize full Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Cleaner Agreement, refund/cancellation policy, evidence retention, liability boundaries, and legal review — so the platform is legally defensible and IC-safe.

**Exit condition:** TOS, IC safeguards, Privacy Policy, and Cleaner Agreement are published and counsel-ready; evidence and liability rules are documented.

**Status:** Design/outline complete for TOS and IC; full consolidation and remaining policies still to be done.

---

## 13.1 Scope (What This Section Covers)

- Full Terms of Service (consolidated document)  
- Independent Contractor Safeguards (already written; merge into TOS + in-app)  
- Cleaner Agreement (separate from TOS)  
- Privacy Policy (GDPR/CPRA framing)  
- Refund & cancellation policy  
- Evidence retention & deletion rules  
- Liability boundaries  
- Legal review pass  

---

## 13.2 Full Terms of Service Consolidation

- Merge all TOS sections produced in the program: Platform role; Independent Contractor relationship; No control over performance; No exclusivity; Service descriptions & outcomes; Evidence & verification; Job completion & payments; Client review & disputes; Platform trust signals & access; Administrative actions; Reservation of rights.  
- Ensure consistency with Section 12 (IC-safe) and Section 11 (admin IC-safe language).  
- **Deliverable:** Single master TOS document ready for counsel.

---

## 13.3 Independent Contractor Safeguards

- **Publish:** Appendix in TOS + in-app copy (cleaner + client).  
- **Content (already written):** What PureTask does not control; what it does define; evidence & verification; platform access; no exclusivity; final statement.  
- **California AB5 / ABC Test:** Prong A (control), B (outside usual course), C (independently established trade) — all satisfied; document in TOS or separate memo for counsel.

---

## 13.4 Cleaner Agreement

- **Separate document** from main TOS (cleaners sign/accept).  
- Covers: IC status; no employment; platform rules; payout terms; dispute resolution; evidence requirements; termination of platform access.  
- **Deliverable:** Cleaner Agreement draft ready for legal review.

---

## 13.5 Privacy Policy

- **Scope:** What data is collected; how it is used; retention; sharing (Stripe, SendGrid, Twilio, etc.); user rights (access, deletion, portability).  
- **GDPR/CPRA framing:** Legal basis; data subject rights; international transfers if applicable.  
- **Deliverable:** Privacy Policy draft ready for legal review.

---

## 13.6 Refund & Cancellation Policy

- **Refund windows:** e.g. 48h after completion; max refund by role (Section 11).  
- **Cancellation:** Client vs cleaner cancellation; fees or credits; grace periods.  
- **Deliverable:** Written policy (user-facing + internal playbook alignment).

---

## 13.7 Evidence Retention & Deletion

- **Retention:** How long job photos, dispute evidence, audit logs are kept.  
- **Deletion:** User deletion requests; automated purge after X months (policy-driven); what must be retained for legal/compliance.  
- **Deliverable:** Evidence retention and deletion policy document.

---

## 13.8 Liability Boundaries

- **Limitations:** Platform liability cap; exclusion of consequential damages; “as is” where appropriate.  
- **Indemnification:** User indemnification of platform for misuse; platform indemnification (if any) for third-party claims.  
- **Insurance:** Cleaner liability; platform insurance (if applicable).  
- **Deliverable:** Liability and indemnification language in TOS; insurance requirements documented.

---

- **Use:** Handoff to counsel for final review and jurisdiction-specific adjustments.
