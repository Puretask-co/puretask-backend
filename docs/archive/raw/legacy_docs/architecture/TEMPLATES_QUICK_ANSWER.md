# ⚡ Templates - Quick Answer

## **What Are Templates?**

**Pre-written messages with blanks that AI fills in automatically.**

---

## **Real Example:**

### **You Write Once:**
```
"Hi {client_name}! Your {service_type} is confirmed 
for {date} at {time}!"
```

### **AI Fills Automatically:**
```
"Hi Sarah! Your Deep Clean is confirmed for 
January 15, 2025 at 2:00 PM!"
```

---

## **Why Use Them?**

- ⚡ **Send messages in 10 seconds** instead of 3 minutes
- ✅ **Never forget important details**
- 💼 **Always sound professional**
- 🎉 **Save 5-10 hours per week!**

---

## **How To Use:**

### **Step 1: Create (One Time)**
```
1. Go to Template Creator
2. Write message with {variables}
3. Click "Save to My Collection"
```

### **Step 2: Use (Forever)**
```
1. Select template
2. AI fills in the blanks
3. Copy & send!
```

**Time:** 10 seconds vs 3 minutes typing!

---

## **Common Templates:**

| Template | When | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| **Booking Confirmation** | New booking | "Hi {client_name}! Confirmed for {date}!" |
| **Running Late** | Traffic/delay | "Running 15 min late, sorry!" |
| **Job Complete** | After cleaning | "All done! Your {property_type} looks amazing!" |
| **Review Request** | After good job | "Would you mind leaving a review? 🙏" |

---

## **How To "Activate":**

```
1. Create template → Saved to your collection
2. Toggle "Active" ON → Now available to use
3. Select when needed → AI fills variables
4. Send! → Done in 10 seconds
```

**That's it!** ✅
