## ✍️ Template Writing Masterclass

### **The CLEAR Framework**

Every great template follows CLEAR:

**C - Clear Purpose**
What is this template for?

**L - Limited Length**
Keep it under 200 characters for SMS

**E - Engaging Tone**
Match your brand personality

**A - Actionable**
Tell client what happens next

**R - Readable**
Easy to understand, no jargon

### **Template Examples: Good vs Great**

#### **Booking Confirmation**

**❌ Bad:**
```
Your booking is confirmed.
```
*Too short, no personality, not helpful*

**✅ Good:**
```
Hi! Your cleaning is confirmed for tomorrow at 2 PM.
```
*Better, but could be more engaging*

**⭐ Great:**
```
Hi {client_name}! 👋 Your cleaning is confirmed for {date} at {time}. 
I'll bring all supplies and can't wait to make your place sparkle! 
See you then! ✨ - {cleaner_name}
```
*Personal, warm, informative, sets expectations*

#### **Running Late**

**❌ Bad:**
```
Running late.
```
*Unprofessional, no details*

**✅ Good:**
```
Running 15 minutes late. Sorry!
```
*Better, but lacks professionalism*

**⭐ Great:**
```
Hi {client_name}! I'm running about {minutes} minutes late due to {reason}. 
My new ETA is {new_eta}. I sincerely apologize for the delay and will still 
complete your full cleaning as scheduled. Thank you for understanding! 🙏
```
*Professional, apologetic, reassuring, includes solution*

#### **Review Request**

**❌ Bad:**
```
Please leave a review.
```
*Demanding, not motivating*

**✅ Good:**
```
If you're happy with my service, I'd appreciate a review!
```
*Better, but not compelling*

**⭐ Great:**
```
Hi {client_name}! I hope you're loving your freshly cleaned space! 🌟 
If you were happy with my service, a quick review would mean the world to me. 
It helps other families find great cleaning services like ours. 
Takes just 30 seconds! Thank you so much for your support! 💙
```
*Personal, explains benefit, makes it easy, shows gratitude*

### **Variable Usage Mastery**

**Essential Variables:**
- `{client_name}` - ALWAYS use for personalization
- `{date}` - For any time-specific message
- `{time}` - Include for appointments
- `{cleaner_name}` - Sign off with your name

**Advanced Variables:**
- `{property_type}` - "your apartment", "your house"
- `{rooms_cleaned}` - "all 3 bedrooms"
- `{amount}` - "$150", be clear about money
- `{eta}` - "15 minutes" for logistics

## 💬 Quick Response Excellence

### **The Perfect Quick Response Formula**

```
[Acknowledge Question] + [Provide Info] + [Next Step] = Perfect Response
```

**Example:**

**Client asks:** *"Do you do deep cleaning?"*

**Formula applied:**
1. **Acknowledge:** "Great question!"
2. **Provide Info:** "Yes, I offer deep cleaning including..."
3. **Next Step:** "Would you like a quote?"

**Result:**
```
Great question! Yes, I offer deep cleaning which includes baseboards, 
inside cabinets, appliances, and detailed bathroom scrubbing - everything 
a basic clean covers plus the extras. Prices start at $X for a Y sq ft home. 
Would you like a detailed quote for your specific space?
```

### **Response Categories Priority**

**Tier 1 (Critical - Must Have):**
- Pricing
- Availability
- Services offered
- Cancellation policy

**Tier 2 (Important - Should Have):**
- Payment methods
- Supplies information
- Pet policies
- Access instructions

**Tier 3 (Helpful - Nice to Have):**
- Tipping policy
- Service frequency options
- Special requests handling
- Background verification

### **Keyword Optimization**

**Do:**
- Use 5-10 keywords per response
- Include variations: "price", "prices", "pricing", "cost", "costs"
- Cover question formats: "how much", "what's the cost", "price for"
- Test with real client questions

**Don't:**
- Use too few keywords (under 3)
- Use only exact phrases
- Ignore variations
- Forget question words (what, how, when, where)

### **Response Length Sweet Spot**

- **Too Short (< 50 chars):** Unhelpful, seems rushed
- **Perfect (100-300 chars):** Informative, readable
- **Too Long (> 500 chars):** Overwhelming, TLDR

**Example Lengths:**

✅ **150 characters:**
```
I bring all supplies including eco-friendly products. If you prefer 
I use your products (allergies/preferences), I'm happy to!
```

✅ **250 characters:**
```
My cancellation policy: FREE cancellation up to 48 hours before. 
Cancellations 24-48 hours in advance = 50% fee. Within 24 hours = 
full charge. This helps me manage my schedule fairly for all clients!
```

## 🎚️ Automation Levels Guide

### **When to Use Each Level**

#### **Manual Mode**
**Use when:**
- ✅ First 1-2 weeks of setup
- ✅ High-value/luxury clients
- ✅ Complex situations requiring judgment
- ✅ You prefer complete control

**Pros:**
- Maximum control
- No surprises
- Learn AI capabilities

**Cons:**
- Time-intensive
- Slower responses
- Defeats AI purpose

**Best for:** New users, luxury market cleaners

#### **Assisted Mode** ⭐ (Recommended)
**Use when:**
- ✅ Learning phase (weeks 3-8)
- ✅ Want oversight with efficiency
- ✅ Building confidence in AI
- ✅ Dealing with diverse clients

**Pros:**
- Balance of control and efficiency
- Catch AI errors before sending
- Learn what works
- Time-saving without risk

**Cons:**
- Still requires checking phone
- Approval bottleneck for urgent messages

**Best for:** Most cleaners, most of the time

#### **Full Automation**
**Use when:**
- ✅ After 2+ months of Assisted Mode
- ✅ Templates are perfected
- ✅ Routine bookings only
- ✅ High trust in system

**Pros:**
- Maximum time savings
- Instant responses
- Scale to more clients
- Focus on cleaning

**Cons:**
- Requires perfect templates
- Risk of mistakes
- Less personal touch
- Must monitor regularly

**Best for:** Experienced users, high-volume cleaners

### **Hybrid Approach (Best of Both Worlds)**

**Strategy:** Use different automation levels for different situations

```
Full Automation:
- Booking confirmations
- Pre-cleaning reminders
- Thank you messages
- Simple availability questions

Assisted Mode:
- Pricing discussions
- Complex scheduling
- First-time client messages
- Special requests

Manual Mode:
- Complaints/issues
- Pricing negotiations
- Relationship-building
- Sensitive situations
```

**How to Set Up:**
1. Go to Settings → Automation Rules
2. Set default level: Assisted
3. Create exceptions:
   - "booking_confirmation" → Full Auto
   - "complaint_keywords" → Manual
   - "first_time_client" → Assisted

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### **Mistake 1: Enabling Full Automation Too Soon**

**Why it's bad:**
- Templates not refined
- AI doesn't know your style yet
- Risk of poor client experiences
- Hard to fix reputation damage

**Solution:**
- Use Assisted Mode for 4-6 weeks minimum
- Customize all templates first
- Test with small client group
- Gradually enable automation

### **Mistake 2: Generic Templates**

**Example of bad template:**
```
Your appointment is confirmed. See you then.
```

**Why it's bad:**
- No personality
- Not memorable
- Misses opportunity to build relationship

**Solution:**
```
Hi {client_name}! 😊 I'm excited to clean your {property_type} on {date}! 
I'll arrive at {time} with all supplies. Can't wait to make your space 
sparkle! ✨ - {cleaner_name}
```

### **Mistake 3: Ignoring Analytics**

**Why it's bad:**
- Can't improve what you don't measure
- Miss opportunities for optimization
- Don't know what's working

**Solution:**
- Check Insights Dashboard weekly
- Note which templates get best responses
- Track booking conversion rate
- Adjust based on data

### **Mistake 4: Set It and Forget It**

**Why it's bad:**
- Client needs change
- Seasons change
- Business evolves
- Templates become stale

**Solution:**
- Review settings monthly
- Update templates quarterly
- Adjust for seasons/holidays
- Stay current

### **Mistake 5: Over-Automation**

**What to NEVER automate:**
- ❌ Complaints/issues
- ❌ Pricing negotiations (complex)
- ❌ Dispute resolution
- ❌ Refund requests
- ❌ Emergency situations

**These NEED human touch!**

### **Mistake 6: Not Personalizing**

**Generic AI = Bad:**
```
Thank you for your booking.
```

**Personalized AI = Good:**
```
Thank you so much, Sarah! I'm excited to clean your beautiful home 
on Tuesday. See you at 2 PM! 🏡✨
```

### **Mistake 7: Wrong Tone for Client Type**

**Mismatch examples:**

❌ Too casual for luxury client:
```
Hey! Gonna clean ur place tmrw! 😎
```

❌ Too formal for casual client:
```
Dear Mrs. Smith, I am writing to confirm the scheduled service...
```

**Solution:** Adjust tone per client type or maintain balanced "Professional & Friendly"

## 🎓 Advanced Strategies

### **Strategy 1: A/B Testing Templates**

**How to do it:**
1. Create 2 versions of important template
2. Use Version A for 2 weeks
3. Use Version B for 2 weeks
4. Compare booking rates
5. Keep winner

**Example:**

**Version A (Professional):**
```
Hi {client_name}, Your cleaning is confirmed for {date} at {time}. 
I look forward to serving you. Thank you for choosing our services.
```

**Version B (Friendly):**
```
Hi {client_name}! 😊 Your cleaning is confirmed for {date} at {time}! 
Can't wait to make your place sparkle! See you soon! ✨
```

**Measure:** Which gets better feedback and repeat bookings?

### **Strategy 2: Seasonal Template Variations**

**Create seasonal versions of top templates:**

**Winter:**
```
Hi {client_name}! ❄️ Your cleaning is confirmed for {date}. 
I'll help get your home cozy and clean for the holidays!
```

**Spring:**
```
Hi {client_name}! 🌸 Spring cleaning time! Your appointment is {date}. 
Let's refresh your space for the new season!
```

**Summer:**
```
Hi {client_name}! ☀️ Your cleaning is confirmed for {date}. 
Looking forward to helping you enjoy a clean, fresh space this summer!
```

**Fall:**
```
Hi {client_name}! 🍂 Your cleaning is confirmed for {date}. 
Let's get your home ready for the cozy fall season!
```

### **Strategy 3: Client Segmentation**

**Create different responses for:**

**New Clients:**
- More informative
- Explain process
- Build trust
- Warmer tone

**Repeat Clients:**
- More casual
- Less explanation
- Friendly and quick
- Relationship-focused

**VIP Clients:**
- Extra personal
- Priority treatment
- Special touches
- Premium feel

### **Strategy 4: Upsell Templates**

**After successful cleaning:**
```
Hi {client_name}! So glad you loved your cleaning! 🌟 
Just wanted to let you know I also offer:
• Deep cleaning services
• Window cleaning
• Organizing help

Would any of these be helpful for you? Happy to provide details!
```

**Result:** 15-20% of clients add services!

### **Strategy 5: Referral Automation**

**After 3+ successful cleanings:**
```
Hi {client_name}! It's been wonderful cleaning for you these past months! 
If you know anyone who could use my services, I'd love to help them too. 
As a thank you, I'll give you both 20% off your next cleaning! 💙
```

**Result:** 25-30% referral rate!
