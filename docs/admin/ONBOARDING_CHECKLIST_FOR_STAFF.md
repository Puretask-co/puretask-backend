# 📋 Cleaner AI Assistant Onboarding Checklist
## For PureTask Staff & Support Team

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This checklist helps you guide new cleaners through AI Assistant setup efficiently. Average onboarding time: **15-20 minutes**.

---

## 📞 Pre-Onboarding (Before Call/Meeting)

**Send to cleaner 24 hours before:**
- [ ] Welcome email with AI overview
- [ ] Link to USER_GUIDE_CLEANER_AI_ASSISTANT.md
- [ ] Link to setup video tutorial
- [ ] Calendar invite for onboarding session

**Prepare:**
- [ ] Test cleaner's account for AI access
- [ ] Verify email/SMS settings are configured
- [ ] Have their profile open
- [ ] Prepare screen share capability

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Introduction (2-3 minutes)

**Say:**
> "Hi [Name]! Today we're going to set up your AI Assistant. It's like having a virtual assistant that responds to clients for you, saving you 5-10 hours per week. The setup takes about 15 minutes. Ready?"

**Explain benefits:**
- ✅ Instant responses (even while cleaning)
- ✅ More bookings (faster response = higher conversion)
- ✅ Professional communication
- ✅ Time savings

**Address concerns:**
- ❓ "Will clients know it's AI?" → "No, messages are sent as you"
- ❓ "What if it makes a mistake?" → "You approve messages in Assisted Mode"
- ❓ "Is it hard to use?" → "No, we'll set it up together now!"

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Cleaner understands AI purpose
- [ ] Benefits clearly explained
- [ ] Concerns addressed
- [ ] Excited to continue

---

## 🎨 Step 2: Personality Setup (5-7 minutes)

**Navigate to:** Settings → AI Assistant Setup Wizard

### **Question 1: Communication Tone**

**Say:**
> "Let's choose how your AI communicates. I recommend 'Professional & Friendly' for most cleaners - it's warm but professional. Does that sound right for you?"

**If they're unsure:**
- Show examples of each tone
- Ask: "How do you normally communicate with clients?"
- Recommend based on their client type:
  - **Luxury homes** → Professional
  - **Families/residential** → Professional & Friendly
  - **Young professionals** → Friendly

**Action:**
- [ ] Help select tone
- [ ] Set formality level (recommend 3)
- [ ] Set emoji usage (recommend Moderate)

### **Question 2: Automation Level**

**Say:**
> "For the first month, I recommend 'Assisted Mode'. This means the AI suggests responses, but you approve them before sending. After you're comfortable, we can enable more automation. Sound good?"

**Important:**
- ⚠️ DO NOT enable Full Automation for new users
- ✅ ALWAYS start with Assisted Mode
- ⏰ Suggest reviewing after 4-6 weeks

**Action:**
- [ ] Select "Assisted Mode"
- [ ] Explain how approval works
- [ ] Show notification settings

### **Question 3: Business Goals**

**Say:**
> "What's most important to you right now: growing your client base, maintaining quality, or work-life balance?"

**Based on answer:**
- **Growing** → "Maximize Bookings"
- **Quality** → "Quality Clients"
- **Unsure** → "Balanced Growth" (default)
- **Preventing burnout** → "Work-Life Balance"

**Action:**
- [ ] Help select goal
- [ ] Set target weekly hours (optional)
- [ ] Explain how AI uses this

### **Question 4: Review & Confirm**

**Say:**
> "Perfect! Let's review your settings. [Read aloud]. Does this sound like you?"

**Action:**
- [ ] Review all selections
- [ ] Make any adjustments
- [ ] Click "Complete Setup"
- [ ] Celebrate: "Great! Your AI personality is set up!"

---

## 📝 Step 3: Template Customization (5-7 minutes)

**Navigate to:** Templates Tab

**Say:**
> "Now let's personalize 3 key templates so they sound like you, not a robot."

### **Template 1: Booking Confirmation**

**Current default:**
```
Hi {client_name}! Your cleaning is confirmed for {date} at {time}. 
I'll bring all supplies and can't wait to make your place sparkle! 
See you then! - {cleaner_name}
```

**Ask:**
> "How would you normally confirm a booking? Let's adjust this to match your style."

**Listen for:**
- Their natural language
- Phrases they use
- Level of enthusiasm

**Action:**
- [ ] Help edit template
- [ ] Keep {variables} intact
- [ ] Test with preview
- [ ] Save changes

### **Template 2: Running Late**

**Say:**
> "Everyone runs late sometimes. Let's make sure this sounds professional and apologetic."

**Action:**
- [ ] Review default template
- [ ] Adjust if needed
- [ ] Ensure it includes apology + new ETA
- [ ] Save

### **Template 3: Review Request**

**Say:**
> "Reviews are gold! This goes out after a successful cleaning. Let's make it compelling."

**Action:**
- [ ] Review default
- [ ] Personalize slightly
- [ ] Keep call-to-action clear
- [ ] Save

**Pro tip:**
> "You can edit more later, but these three are the most important!"

## 💬 Step 4: Quick Responses (3 minutes)

**Navigate to:** Quick Responses Tab

**Say:**
> "You already have 18 pre-written responses to common questions. Let's look at the top 3."

### **Review Together:**

1. **Pricing Response**
   - [ ] Read it together
   - [ ] Ask: "Does this match your pricing approach?"
   - [ ] Edit if needed

2. **Availability Response**
   - [ ] Verify it reflects their typical availability
   - [ ] Adjust if necessary

3. **Services Response**
   - [ ] Confirm it lists their actual services
   - [ ] Update if needed

**Action:**
- [ ] Mark these 3 as favorites ⭐
- [ ] Explain: "When clients ask these questions, AI suggests these responses"
- [ ] Show trigger keywords

**Say:**
> "Feel free to add your own later. For now, these cover 80% of questions!"

---

## ⚙️ Step 5: Key Settings (2 minutes)

**Navigate to:** Settings Tab

**Verify these are ON:**
- [ ] Booking Confirmation (enabled)
- [ ] Pre-Cleaning Reminder (24 hours, enabled)
- [ ] New Booking Alerts (enabled)
- [ ] Daily Summary (enabled)

**Verify these are OFF initially:**
- [ ] Full Automation (OFF - using Assisted)
- [ ] AI Schedule Optimization (OFF for now)

**Say:**
> "These settings ensure you get notified of all AI activity and can review suggestions."

## 📱 Step 6: Mobile Setup (2 minutes)

**Help them:**
- [ ] Download PureTask app (if not already)

**Walk through:**
1. "When AI suggests a response, you'll get a notification"
2. "Tap it to see the suggestion"
3. "You can edit, approve, or decline"
4. "It's that simple!"

## 🎓 Step 7: Test Together (2-3 minutes)

**Say:**
> "Let's do a quick test to make sure everything works!"

**Test scenarios:**

1. **Booking Confirmation Test**
   - [ ] Create test booking (or use recent one)
   - [ ] Show AI generates confirmation
   - [ ] Have them approve it
   - [ ] Verify delivery

2. **Quick Response Test**
   - [ ] Ask: "How much do you charge?"
   - [ ] Show AI suggests pricing response
   - [ ] Have them review/approve

**Action:**
- [ ] Cleaner successfully approved a suggestion
- [ ] They understand the workflow
- [ ] Comfortable with process

## ⚠️ Common Issues & Solutions

### **Issue: "I'm not getting AI suggestions"**
**Check:**
- [ ] Notifications enabled?
- [ ] AI not in Manual Mode?
- [ ] Any bookings/messages to respond to?

**Solution:** Walk through notification settings

### **Issue: "AI sounds too formal/casual"**
**Check:**
- [ ] Current tone setting
- [ ] Formality level
- [ ] Emoji usage

**Solution:** Adjust in Settings → Communication

### **Issue: "I don't trust AI to respond"**
**Check:**
- [ ] Currently in Assisted Mode?
- [ ] Understand they approve everything?

**Solution:** Reassure about control, offer extra templates review

### **Issue: "Not saving time yet"**
**Check:**
- [ ] How long have they used it?
- [ ] Are they approving suggestions?
- [ ] Templates customized?

**Solution:** If < 2 weeks, normal. If > 2 weeks, review their usage patterns

## 💡 Pro Tips for Staff

1. **Keep it simple** - Don't overwhelm with features
2. **Show, don't tell** - Demo everything live
3. **Be encouraging** - This is new for many cleaners
4. **Customize approach** - Tech-savvy vs beginners need different pace
5. **Use their language** - Avoid technical jargon
6. **Celebrate success** - "You're going to love this!"
7. **Follow up** - Week 4 check-in is crucial
8. **Document issues** - Help improve onboarding
