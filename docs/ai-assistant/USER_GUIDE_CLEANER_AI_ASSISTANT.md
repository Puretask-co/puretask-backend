## 🤖 What is Your AI Assistant?

Think of your AI Assistant as your **personal virtual assistant** that:

- ✅ **Responds to client messages** when you're busy
- ✅ **Sends booking confirmations** automatically
- ✅ **Reminds clients** before appointments
- ✅ **Answers common questions** using your style
- ✅ **Manages your schedule** based on your preferences
- ✅ **Learns from you** to get better over time

### **What Your AI Does NOT Do:**
- ❌ Make decisions without your approval (unless you enable full automation)
- ❌ Share your personal information
- ❌ Replace you - it's a helper, not a replacement!

## 🚀 Getting Started

### **Step 1: Set Up Your AI Personality** (5 minutes)

When you first access your AI settings, you'll go through a quick setup wizard:

#### **Question 1: How should your AI communicate?**

Choose your style:

- **Professional** 👔
  - Best for: Corporate clients, luxury homes
  - Example: "Good afternoon! Your cleaning appointment is confirmed for tomorrow."
  - Tone: Formal, business-like

- **Friendly** 😊
  - Best for: Regular clients, families with kids
  - Example: "Hey there! Can't wait to make your place sparkle tomorrow! 🌟"
  - Tone: Warm, personal

- **Professional & Friendly** 🤝 **(Recommended)**
  - Best for: Most situations
  - Example: "Hi Sarah! Your cleaning is confirmed for tomorrow at 2 PM. Looking forward to it!"
  - Tone: Balanced, approachable yet professional

- **Casual** 😎
  - Best for: Close relationships, informal clients
  - Example: "Hey! All set for tomorrow. See you then! 😊"
  - Tone: Relaxed, conversational

**💡 Tip:** Start with "Professional & Friendly" - you can always change it later!

#### **Question 2: How much should your AI do automatically?**

Choose your automation level:

- **Manual Mode** ✋
  - **What happens:** AI suggests responses, you approve everything
  - **Best for:** You want complete control
  - **Example:** Client asks about pricing → AI drafts response → You review and send

- **Assisted Mode** 🤝 **(Recommended for beginners)**
  - **What happens:** AI handles routine tasks, asks for important decisions
  - **Best for:** Most cleaners
  - **Example:** Booking confirmations sent automatically, but pricing questions wait for your approval

- **Full Automation** 🚀
  - **What happens:** AI handles everything automatically
  - **Best for:** Experienced users who trust the system
  - **Example:** All messages, bookings, and confirmations handled without your input

**💡 Tip:** Start with Assisted Mode for a few weeks, then consider Full Automation if comfortable!

#### **Question 3: What's your business goal?**

Choose what matters most:

- **Maximize Bookings** 📈
  - AI will: Accept more jobs, fill schedule quickly
  - Best for: Growing your business fast

- **Quality Clients** ⭐
  - AI will: Focus on high-value, reliable clients
  - Best for: Established cleaners with steady income

- **Balanced Growth** ⚖️ **(Recommended)**
  - AI will: Mix of quantity and quality
  - Best for: Most cleaners

- **Work-Life Balance** 🧘
  - AI will: Respect your hours, prevent burnout
  - Best for: Maintaining healthy boundaries

**💡 Tip:** Choose "Balanced Growth" unless you have specific goals!

#### **Question 4: Review & Confirm**

Review all your choices and click **"Complete Setup"**. You're done! 🎉

## 💬 Understanding Your AI Personality

### **Communication Settings**

#### **Formality Level** (1-5 scale)

Move the slider to control how formal your AI sounds:

```
1 (Very Casual) ←────────→ 5 (Very Formal)
```

**Examples:**

| Level | Example Message |
|-------|-----------------|
| **1 - Very Casual** | "Hey! Your place looks great! See ya next time! 😎" |
| **2 - Casual** | "Hi there! Everything's clean and ready. Have a great day!" |
| **3 - Balanced** | "Hi Sarah! I've finished cleaning your home. Everything looks wonderful!" |
| **4 - Formal** | "Good afternoon, Mrs. Johnson. Your cleaning service has been completed." |
| **5 - Very Formal** | "Dear Mrs. Johnson, I am writing to confirm the successful completion of your scheduled cleaning service." |

**💡 Tip:** Most clients prefer levels 2-3!

#### **Emoji Usage**

Choose how often your AI uses emojis:

- **None** 🚫 - No emojis (very professional)
- **Minimal** 😐 - Occasional (1-2 per message)
- **Moderate** 🙂 - Regular use (3-5 per message) **(Recommended)**
- **Frequent** 😄 - Lots of emojis (5+ per message)

**Examples:**

| Setting | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **None** | "Your cleaning is confirmed for tomorrow at 2 PM." |
| **Minimal** | "Your cleaning is confirmed for tomorrow at 2 PM. See you then! 😊" |
| **Moderate** | "Hi! 👋 Your cleaning is confirmed for tomorrow at 2 PM. Can't wait to make your place sparkle! ✨" |
| **Frequent** | "Hey there! 👋😊 Your cleaning is confirmed for tomorrow at 2 PM! 🕑 Can't wait to make your place sparkle clean! ✨🧹💫" |

**💡 Tip:** Moderate emoji use makes messages friendly without being unprofessional!

## 📝 Managing Message Templates

Templates are pre-written messages your AI uses for common situations.

### **Default Templates You Have:**

1. **Booking Confirmation** ✅
   - When: New booking made
   - Example: "Hi {client_name}! Your cleaning is confirmed for {date} at {time}."

2. **Pre-Cleaning Reminder** ⏰
   - When: 24 hours before cleaning
   - Example: "Hi! Just a friendly reminder about tomorrow's cleaning at {time}."

3. **On My Way** 🚗
   - When: Heading to client location
   - Example: "I'm on my way! ETA: {eta} minutes."

4. **Job Complete** ✨
   - When: Cleaning finished
   - Example: "All done! Your {property_type} is sparkling clean!"

5. **Review Request** ⭐
   - When: 24 hours after service
   - Example: "Hi! If you're happy with the service, a quick review would mean so much!"

6. **Running Late** 🕐
   - When: Delayed arrival
   - Example: "Hi! Running {minutes} minutes late due to {reason}. New ETA: {new_eta}."

7. **Payment Thank You** 💰
   - When: Payment received
   - Example: "Thank you for your payment of {amount}! Pleasure serving you!"

8. **Rescheduling** 📅
   - When: Need to change appointment
   - Example: "Hi! I need to reschedule our {original_date} appointment. Available: {alternative_dates}."

...and **6 more** professional templates!

### **How to Edit a Template:**

1. Go to **"Templates"** tab
2. Click **"Edit"** on any template
3. Change the text (keep the `{variables}` intact!)
4. Click **"Preview"** to see how it looks
5. Click **"Save"**

**💡 Template Variables:**

Variables are replaced with actual information:

| Variable | Becomes |
|----------|---------|
| `{client_name}` | "Sarah" |
| `{date}` | "January 10th" |
| `{time}` | "2:00 PM" |
| `{cleaner_name}` | Your name |
| `{address}` | Client's address |
| `{property_type}` | "apartment", "house", etc. |
| `{amount}` | "$150" |

**Example:**

**Template:** `Hi {client_name}! See you at {time}!`

**Becomes:** `Hi Sarah! See you at 2:00 PM!`

### **Creating Your Own Template:**

1. Click **"+ Add New Template"**
2. Choose template type (booking, reminder, etc.)
3. Give it a name: "My Custom Confirmation"
4. Write your message (use variables by clicking them)
5. Test with sample data
6. Save!

**💡 Pro Tip:** Keep templates under 200 characters for SMS compatibility!

## 💬 Quick Responses Library

Quick Responses are pre-written answers to common questions. Your AI suggests these when clients ask similar questions.

### **Default Quick Responses You Have:**

#### **1. Pricing** 💰
**Trigger words:** price, cost, rate, how much

**Response:** "My rates depend on home size and cleaning type. For a quote, could you share your home size (sq ft or bedrooms/bathrooms) and type of cleaning needed? I offer basic, deep, and move-out cleaning options."

#### **2. Availability** 📅
**Trigger words:** available, schedule, when, appointment

**Response:** "I'd be happy to help! What date and time works best for you? I typically have openings throughout the week."

#### **3. Cancellation Policy** ❌
**Trigger words:** cancel, cancellation, refund, policy

**Response:** "FREE cancellation up to 48 hours before. 24-48 hours = 50% fee. Within 24 hours = full charge. This helps me manage my schedule fairly for all clients!"

#### **4. Pet-Friendly** 🐾
**Trigger words:** pet, dog, cat, animal

**Response:** "I love pets! 🐾 I'm comfortable with dogs, cats, and other animals. For safety, please secure pets in a separate room if possible. Let me know about any aggressive behaviors!"

#### **5. Supplies** 🧴
**Trigger words:** supplies, products, bring, equipment

**Response:** "I bring all supplies including eco-friendly products, vacuum, mop, and cloths. If you prefer I use your products (allergies/preferences), I'm happy to!"

...and **13 more** comprehensive responses covering:
- Payment methods
- Special requests
- Access/parking instructions
- Tipping policy
- Service frequency
- Time estimates
- What's included
- And more!

### **How Quick Responses Work:**

1. Client messages: *"Do you bring your own supplies?"*
2. AI detects keywords: "supplies", "bring"
3. AI suggests the "Supplies" quick response
4. In **Assisted Mode:** You review and send
5. In **Full Automation:** AI sends automatically

### **Creating Your Own Quick Response:**

1. Go to **"Quick Responses"** tab
2. Click **"+ Add Response"**
3. Choose category (pricing, availability, etc.)
4. Add trigger keywords: `price, cost, rate`
5. Write your response (max 500 characters)
6. Save!

**💡 Pro Tips:**
- Mark frequently used responses as **Favorite** ⭐ for quick access
- Update responses based on actual client questions you receive
- Keep responses friendly and helpful

## 📖 Common Scenarios

### **Scenario 1: Client Asks About Pricing**

**What Happens:**

1. Client messages: *"How much do you charge for a 3-bedroom house?"*
2. AI detects: Keywords "how much", "charge", "3-bedroom"
3. AI suggests: Your "Pricing" quick response

**In Assisted Mode:**
- You see: *"AI suggests: 'My rates depend on...'"*
- You can: Edit, approve, or write custom response
- You click: "Send"

**In Full Automation:**
- AI sends the pricing response immediately
- You get notification: "AI responded to pricing question"

### **Scenario 2: New Booking Made**

**What Happens:**

1. Client books cleaning for tomorrow at 2 PM
2. AI activates: "Booking Confirmation" template
3. AI fills in: Client name, date (tomorrow), time (2 PM)
4. AI sends: "Hi Sarah! Your cleaning is confirmed for tomorrow at 2 PM..."

**You Get:**
- Email notification: "Booking confirmed for Sarah Johnson"
- Calendar update with appointment
- SMS reminder 1 hour before

### **Scenario 3: Running Late**

**What Happens:**

1. You're stuck in traffic, running 20 minutes late
2. You click: "Quick Action → Running Late"
3. AI uses: "Running Late" template
4. AI fills in: Minutes late (20), reason (traffic), new ETA
5. AI sends: "Hi! Running about 20 minutes late due to traffic. New ETA: 2:20 PM. Sorry!"

**Pro Tip:** Set this up as a one-tap shortcut on your phone!

### **Scenario 4: Client Complains**

**What Happens:**

1. Client messages: *"You missed cleaning the bathroom mirror"*
2. AI detects: Negative sentiment, specific issue
3. **AI ALWAYS forwards to you** (too important to automate)
4. You see: *"Client issue - needs your attention"*
5. You respond personally: "I'm so sorry! I'll come back tomorrow to fix that!"

**Important:** AI never handles complaints automatically!

### **Scenario 5: Vacation Mode**

**What Happens:**

1. You're going on vacation next week
2. You activate: "Vacation Auto-Reply" template
3. You set: Start date, end date, backup contact
4. AI sends: "Thanks for reaching out! I'm on vacation {dates}. Back soon!"
5. All new messages get auto-reply

**Pro Tip:** Set this up 3 days before vacation!

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### **Problem: AI Sounds Too Formal**

**Solution:**
1. Go to Settings → Communication Tone
2. Lower formality level (try level 2-3)
3. Change tone to "Friendly" or "Professional & Friendly"
4. Increase emoji usage to "Moderate"

### **Problem: AI Responds Too Quickly**

**Solution:**
1. Go to Settings → Response Speed
2. Change from "Immediate" to "Balanced" or "Thoughtful"
3. Consider switching from Full to Assisted mode

### **Problem: Templates Don't Sound Like Me**

**Solution:**
1. Go to Templates tab
2. Click "Edit" on each template
3. Rewrite in your own words (keep the {variables})
4. Save and test with preview

### **Problem: Too Many/Too Few Bookings**

**Solution:**

**Too Many:**
1. Go to Settings → Priority Goal
2. Change to "Work-Life Balance"
3. Set target weekly hours
4. Enable "Decline Outside Hours"

**Too Few:**
1. Change priority goal to "Maximize Bookings"
2. Enable "Auto-Accept Instant Book"
3. Expand preferred client types
4. Lower pricing slightly in your profile

### **Problem: Missing Important Messages**

**Solution:**
1. Go to Settings → Notifications
2. Turn ON "New Booking Alerts"
3. Enable "Daily Summary"
4. Check spam folder for AI notifications

### **Problem: AI Made a Mistake**

**Solution:**
1. Immediately: Send personal message to client apologizing
2. Go to AI settings and switch to Assisted Mode temporarily
3. Report the issue (helps us improve AI)
4. Consider if template/response needs updating

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### **General Questions**

**Q: Does the AI cost extra?**
A: No! AI Assistant is included free with your PureTask account.

**Q: Can I turn off the AI completely?**
A: Yes! Set all settings to "Manual Mode" or toggle OFF individual features.

**Q: Will clients know it's AI responding?**
A: No. Messages are sent as if you wrote them. But be honest if asked!

**Q: What happens if AI makes a mistake?**
A: You can override any AI action and send a correction. Always supervise AI, especially at first.

**Q: Can I use my own templates?**
A: Absolutely! You can edit all templates or create custom ones.

### **Privacy & Security**

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. All messages are encrypted and your data never leaves PureTask servers.

**Q: Can AI access my personal information?**
A: AI only accesses information needed for bookings: your name, service details, schedule. Never bank info or passwords.

**Q: What data does AI learn from?**
A: Only your message templates, quick responses, and booking patterns. You can disable learning in Settings → Privacy.

**Q: Can I delete my AI data?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Privacy → "Delete All AI Data" (this resets everything to defaults).

### **Automation Questions**

**Q: What's the difference between Assisted and Full Automation?**
A:
- **Assisted:** AI suggests, you approve
- **Full:** AI acts automatically, you get notifications

**Q: Should I use Full Automation?**
A: Only after 2-4 weeks with Assisted Mode. Make sure templates are perfect first!

**Q: What if I'm on Full Automation and need to pause it?**
A: Click "Emergency Stop" in settings or switch to Manual Mode instantly.

**Q: Can AI book cleaning appointments without asking me?**
A: Only if you enable "Full Automation" AND "Auto-Accept Bookings". By default, bookings need approval.

### **Templates & Responses**

**Q: How many templates can I have?**
A: Unlimited! But 10-15 quality templates cover most needs.

**Q: Can I share templates with other cleaners?**
A: Not yet, but this feature is coming soon!

**Q: What if I want AI to stop using a template?**
A: Toggle it to "Inactive" in the Templates tab. It's saved but not used.

**Q: Can I use emojis in templates?**
A: Yes! Just type them normally. Some systems (SMS) may not support all emojis though.

### **Performance & Optimization**

**Q: How do I know if my AI is working well?**
A: Check the Insights Dashboard weekly. Look for:
- High usage counts (templates being used)
- Good response rates from clients
- Few manual overrides needed

**Q: My AI isn't getting many bookings. Why?**
A: Could be:
- Priority goal set too conservatively
- Pricing too high
- Limited availability in schedule
- Need to expand service areas

**Q: How often should I update my settings?**
A: Review monthly, adjust as needed. Your business changes, your AI should too!

**Q: Can I test changes before applying them?**
A: Yes! Use the "Preview" feature in Template Editor to see how messages will look.
