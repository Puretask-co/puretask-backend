**Liability boundaries**

- **Limitation of liability** and **indemnification** are defined in [TOS_CONSOLIDATED.md](./TOS_CONSOLIDATED.md). Do not expose the platform to unlimited liability; cap damages as agreed with counsel.
- **Cleaner Agreement** [CLEANER_AGREEMENT.md](./CLEANER_AGREEMENT.md) clarifies IC status, platform rules, and payouts; liability as between platform and cleaner should align with TOS and local law.
- For any new product or high-risk feature, confirm liability and indemnity language with legal before launch.

**Legal review checklist (counsel sign-off)**

Before production launch, counsel should review and sign off (or note exceptions) on:

- [ ] **TOS_CONSOLIDATED.md** — single master TOS (IC, evidence, disputes, admin, liability, indemnity).
- [ ] **PRIVACY_POLICY.md** — GDPR/CPRA framing, retention, data use, evidence retention.
- [ ] **CLEANER_AGREEMENT.md** — IC status, platform rules, payouts, liability.
- [ ] **In-app copy** — [IN_APP_COPY_CLEANER.md](./IN_APP_COPY_CLEANER.md), [IN_APP_COPY_CLIENT.md](./IN_APP_COPY_CLIENT.md); no employment-like promises or mandates.
- [ ] **Evidence retention** — where and how long photos, logs, dispute evidence are kept; legal hold process.
- [ ] **Refund policy** — refund windows, cancellation fees, alignment with TOS and ledger (RUNBOOK § 3.5).
- [ ] **AB5 / IC analysis** — [AB5_ANALYSIS.md](./AB5_ANALYSIS.md) and [IC_SAFEGUARDS_APPENDIX.md](./IC_SAFEGUARDS_APPENDIX.md) consistent with product and operations.
