# In-App Copy — Cleaner (IC-Safe Bullets)

**Purpose:** Short, IC-safe copy for the cleaner app. Use in onboarding, help, and dispute/evidence flows.  
**Last updated:** 2026-02-02

---

## Onboarding

- You’re an independent contractor. You choose when to work and how to perform services.
- PureTask connects you with clients. We don’t employ you or tell you how to clean.
- You can work for other platforms and clients. No exclusivity.
- You’re responsible for your own taxes, insurance, and tools.

---

## Evidence & Photos

- Photos help verify that service outcomes were delivered and support dispute resolution.
- Submitting evidence can qualify you for platform protections (e.g., payout protection).
- You choose whether to provide evidence; some protections depend on it.
- Guidelines describe what’s useful (e.g., before/after, relevant areas). They’re not work instructions.

---

## Reliability & Access

- Reliability reflects your platform interaction history (completions, evidence, disputes, cancellations).
- It affects access to job visibility, payout timing, and platform protections.
- It does not evaluate how you clean or impose employment-like supervision.
- Adjustments to access are participation conditions, not discipline.

---

## Disputes

- If a client disputes a job, we review evidence and outcomes to resolve it fairly.
- You can submit evidence to support your side. Decisions are based on marketplace standards.
- Resolutions (refund, no refund) are transaction outcomes, not performance evaluations.
