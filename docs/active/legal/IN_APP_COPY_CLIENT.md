# In-App Copy — Client (IC-Safe Bullets)

**Purpose:** Short, IC-safe copy for the client app. Use in booking, review, and dispute flows.  
**Last updated:** 2026-02-02

---

## Service & Outcomes

- You purchase a cleaning service with defined outcomes: areas included, tasks expected, exclusions.
- Cleaners are independent contractors. They choose how to deliver those outcomes.
- PureTask facilitates the connection and handles payments and disputes.

---

## Review & Completion

- After the job, you have a review window to confirm completion or report concerns.
- If you don’t take action, the job is auto-accepted and the cleaner is paid.
- You can file a dispute within the dispute window (e.g., 48 hours of completion).

---

## Disputes

- Disputes are reviewed by comparing what you purchased, evidence provided, and your feedback.
- Outcomes include full refund, partial refund, or no refund, depending on the facts.
- Decisions are made to fairly resolve the transaction. Evidence from both sides is considered.

---

## Evidence

- Photos and evidence document outcomes, not how the cleaner worked.
- Evidence helps resolve disputes objectively and protect both you and the cleaner.
- Our dispute process uses marketplace standards to reach fair outcomes.
